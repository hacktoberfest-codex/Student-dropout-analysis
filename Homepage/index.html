ABOUT US

Our model is to predict whether a student is at risk of dropping out. Student dropout rates have significant social and economic implications. By identifying and addressing the factors leading to dropouts, we can improve educational outcomes and create a brighter future for students. The model will then be used to predict the probability of a student dropping out, and this information can be used to intervene early and prevent dropout.
